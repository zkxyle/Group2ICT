The Relevance of Websites in Our Modern World.

A website is a collection of related webpages in which is identified through a single domain name. And as we move forward into modernization, accessing sites are easier with the help of the internet. Websites, with its different functions, play an important role in our modern society. It is used mainly in dissseminating information, businesses, social networking, and even in entertainment. With these purposes, communication and information dissemination have been easier than before, making us in this time, more technology-relient in every other field. Its purpose and role in our society is undeniably relevant in making our lives way easier and accessible than before. But with this advantages comes with negative sides that we need to look out for in order to use it at its best, prioritizing our safety and well-beings as individuals who live in a modern society.
